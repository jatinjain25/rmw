Our Story

Three Generations.
One Standard.

Rajasthan Marble World was founded in 1999 with a simple belief — that a stone slab is never just material. It is the floor a family will live on for thirty years, the counter a kitchen is built around, the vanity a guest will remember.

Twenty-five years on, we still source every slab the same way: in person, by family, slab by slab. From Italian quarries to Rajasthani workshops to two showrooms in Hyderabad.
