Our Story

Three Generations. One Standard.

From the marble quarries of Rajasthan to two showrooms in Hyderabad — twenty-five years of curating stone, one slab at a time.

Heritage

A family business, built on stone.

Rajasthan Marble World was founded in 1999 by the Jain family, carrying forward a stone-trading heritage that began in the workshops of Rajasthan generations earlier. What started as a single Hyderabad showroom has become two — but the way we work hasn't changed.

Every slab we sell is chosen in person. We don't buy from catalogs or ship sight-unseen. We travel to the quarries, walk the warehouses, and select the pieces that meet our standard for color, vein, finish, and longevity.

The result is a curated catalog of Italian, Indian, and imported marble and granite — material we'd put in our own homes, offered to yours.
